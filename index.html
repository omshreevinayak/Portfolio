Research & Discovery

Through extensive research and discovery, I analyzed numerous hotel booking websites and identified a critical gap in their complaint resolution processes. I found that when users register complaints, resolving these issues is left solely to the hotels, with no intervention from the booking platform. This lack of oversight can lead to unresolved complaints and negatively impact the platform's reputation. To address this, I developed a Dual-Path Complaint Resolution system. This innovative system ensures that complaints are sent to both the hotel and the website owner, guaranteeing that user issues are monitored and resolved promptly, thereby enhancing customer satisfaction and maintaining the platform's credibility.
