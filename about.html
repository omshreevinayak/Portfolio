Project Title: Dual-Path Complaint Resolution System For Enhancing Responsiveness in Hotel Booking World.

Area/Domain of Project: Web Development / Hospitality Management

ABSTRACT:

In the competitive hospitality industry, customer satisfaction is paramount for the success and longevity of any booking platform. Traditional hotel booking websites like OYO often fall short when addressing user complaints, as these issues are directed solely to the hotel management. This can lead to unresolved problems, resulting in a tarnished reputation for the booking platform. The "Dual-Path Complaint Resolution: Enhancing Responsiveness in Hotel Booking Systems" project aims to bridge this gap by introducing a novel feature where user complaints are simultaneously sent to both the hotel administration and the website owner. This dual-notification system ensures a higher likelihood of issue resolution, thereby enhancing the overall user experience.

The primary problem this project addresses is the lack of accountability and responsiveness in current booking systems. When user complaints are only directed to the hotel, there is no guarantee that the issue will be resolved promptly or adequately. This lack of oversight can negatively impact customer trust and satisfaction, as users may feel neglected or ignored. By implementing a system where complaints are also sent to the website owner, there is an additional layer of accountability, ensuring that issues are more likely to be addressed and resolved efficiently.

This project seeks to fill the gap in existing customer service protocols within hotel booking platforms. The current one-directional complaint system often leaves users dissatisfied and can harm the reputation of the booking website. The dual-notification system not only holds the hotel accountable but also allows the website owner to monitor the quality of service provided by the hotels listed on their platform. This added transparency and responsiveness are crucial for maintaining a high standard of customer service.

The outcomes of this study are significant as they offer a practical solution to improve customer relations and maintain the integrity of the booking website. By addressing the issue of unresolved complaints, the project enhances user satisfaction and fosters trust in the platform. This is crucial for sustaining a competitive advantage in the digital hospitality market, where customer loyalty is key. By ensuring that user complaints are addressed promptly and effectively, the "Dual-Path Complaint Resolution: Enhancing Responsiveness in Hotel Booking Systems" can differentiate itself from competitors and build a loyal customer base, ultimately contributing to long-term success.
